Recipe for Hot chocolate:

Ingredients required:

  • Milk (dairy or alternative)
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Sugar (white granulated or brown)
  • Salt
  • Water (to make a paste with the dry ingredients)
  • Chocolate chips (semi-sweet or bittersweet)
  • Vanilla extract


list of steps to make hot chocolate:
  1. In a small saucepan, add:
    1. Unsweetened cocoa powder
    2. Sugar
    3. Salt
  2. Add a little water (just enough to make a smooth paste).
  3. Stir over medium heat until the mixture forms a thick, smooth paste and starts to bubble slightly.
  4. Pour in the milk (dairy or alternative).
  5. Stir continuously while heating, but do not boil.
  6. Once the milk is hot, add chocolate chips.
  7. Stir until they melt completely and the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  8. Remove from heat, then stir in vanilla extract.
